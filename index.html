Last Updated: 02.04.2025

1. Introduction

Mobile Football ("the App") is designed for football enthusiasts and offers interactive content, blog posts, video sharing, quizzes, and prediction games. This Privacy Policy explains the types of personal data we collect, how we use and store that data, and under what circumstances it may be shared with third parties. By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information Collected

Personal Information

When you register or sign in, we may collect personal information such as your email address, username, and profile details. This information is collected through services like Firebase Authentication.

User-Generated Content

Any content you create—including blog posts, video uploads, comments, likes, and votes—is subject to a manual review process via our admin panel before it is made publicly available.

Device and Usage Information

We automatically collect technical data, such as device model, operating system, IP address, and usage statistics, to improve the performance and user experience of the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and enhance the services of the App.

  • To protect against fraud, misuse, and unauthorized access.
  • For analytics and statistical reporting purposes.
  • To process user notifications and reports.

4. Data Sharing

Your personal data will not be shared with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental request.
  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party providers such as Firebase, to help deliver and maintain our services.
  • Business Partners: In limited cases where data is shared with partners for service improvement purposes, and only after ensuring the data is handled securely.

5. Security

We prioritize the security of your personal information. Our App implements industry-standard encryption, access controls, and other security measures to protect your data. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. Any data that is no longer needed will be securely deleted.
